About Our Dojo

Our dojo is located in Mirpur-10 Shaheed Suhrawardi Indoor Stadium. There are many dojo out their but our dojo is the best. We have regullarly 6 day traning in a week. Our morning schedule start from 7 am to 8:30 am and evening schedule start from 4:30 pm to 6:00 pm. We also provide personal traning at peoples home and we also provide female trainer. We also train outside of Shaheed Suhrawardi Indoor Stadium. Our main style is wado ryo karate . Beside karate we also train style of martialart like kickboxing. Our dojo registered in Bangladesh karate fedaration and Bangladesh martialart con fedaration. We have over 100 students and the number of student is increasing day by day.
